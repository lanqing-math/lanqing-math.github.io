Research Interests

I work in geometric topology. I am interested in 3-manifolds, knot theory and hyperbolic geometry. In particular I am currently working on representations of fundamental groups of 3-manifolds and their invariants, with the help of Mathematica, SnapPy, Regina, SageMath, Magma, GAP, and various other tools. I also have broader interests in other aspects of low‑dimensional topology and algebraic methods.
